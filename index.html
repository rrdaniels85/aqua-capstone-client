Aquarium Tracker


About the Application

We seek to support aquarium and aquatic animal enthusiasts everywhere with this innovative application. This application allows a user to monitor multiple aquariums and keep an inventory of the aquatic life living in each tank.




About the Creator

Rachel Daniels is a web developer. She has had a number of pet fish in her life over the years, from her first pet Abraham (the black goldfish), to her perpetually angry betta fish, Spade. She never truly appreciated how much care went into these little creatures until she got an aquarium as an adult.

As a result, this project is dedicated to her parents who she wants to thank for cleaning her aquarium for many, many, ... many years during her adolescence.
